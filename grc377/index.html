Little Beadies aims to take a step back from the busyness of life and remind people that we can treasure earth wherever we go.

Through our authentic beaded products, we hope to inspire people to appreciate the natural beauty of the earth.

We are located in Downtown San Luis Obispo.
Come experience nature in beaded form!
